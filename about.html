About Us

Who We Are

The World Amputee Crutches Athletic Federation is a global organization dedicated to promoting amputee sports for athletes who compete using crutches. We aim to build a thriving community where athletes of all ages and abilities can discover their strength, develop their skills, and compete with pride.
