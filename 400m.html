  • 400m track event

The 400-meter crutches track event is a full-lap race around the track, demanding both endurance and pacing. Athletes begin from staggered starting positions, using crutches to generate forward momentum while managing their energy across the full distance. This event requires athletes to find a sustainable rhythm, balancing speed with control to avoid fatigue. As they come off the final curve, they push into the home stretch, using every ounce of strength for a powerful finish. The 400m is a challenging race that combines stamina, precision, and determination, showcasing the athletes’ resilience and grit over a longer distance.
